Thomas Alva Edison

The man who failed 10000 times

  • 1847 - Born on February 11, 1847 in Milan, Ohio
  • 1866 - Works as telegraph operator at Western Union
  • 1867 - Fired from Western Union for requesting night shift
  • 1869 - Patents the Electric Vote Recorder
  • 1876 - Opens First Industrial Research Lab
  • 1877 - Completes the Model for The First Phonograph
  • 1878 - Patents the First Phonograph
  • 1879 - Improves Carbon Lamp
  • 1880 - Patents DC Electricity Distribution
  • 1880 - Founds the Edison Illuminating Company
  • 1883 - Establishes Edison & Swan United Electric Light Company
  • 1892 - General Electric is Established
  • 1892 - Patents Two Way Telegraph
  • 1897 - Patents The Kinetographic Camera
  • 1931 - Dies at he age of 84

I haven't failed.
I've just found 10,000 ways
that won't work.

- Edison
